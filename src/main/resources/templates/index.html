Welcome to the Cassandra Lan Party Configurer

This application will help you to setup Cassandra on your laptop

If you have any problem or any doubt, please call a team member. We are there for that!

At this stage, You should :

be sitting more or less confortably
have turned off your WIFI
have a sticky note on the wall with your name and your IP
have configured your network parametrers
be connected to the switch with a network cable
have a jdk 1.8 installed on your laptop
java -version (download of jdk 1.8)
be able to ping those addresses :
ping 10.1.1.1
ping 10.2.1.1
ping 10.3.1.1
Your IP is :
If it's not the one written on your sticky note, call a team member
Start the installation and the configuration of Cassandra
